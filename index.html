I work at The Wall Street Journal as a Visual Correspondent.

My work is focused on the visual exploration of the patterns around us in technology, nature and culture.

Previously, I was the Director of Interactive Graphics for the Journal, and a news app developer before that. I also had a good run as a freelance illustrator.

I have worked for the Journal for more than 15 years, and have been writing code, designing, illustrating and making things since graduating from Syracuse University with a BFA in 1995.

I live in the Crown Heights neighborhood of Brooklyn, NY.
